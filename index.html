Biography

I am now a last year M.E candidate at Department of Automation, Tsinghua University, China and work with Prof. Yi Li in the Multiphase Flow Measurement Lab, Institute of Measurement and Electronic Technology. Before that I have worked as a Control Engineer in a power plant for four years. I recived my B.E degree from School of Control and Computer Engineering, North China Electric Power University, Beijing, China in Jul. 2011.

My current research interest broadly includes image reconstruction, electrical tomography, multi-sensor data analysis and their applications to multiphase flow measurement. If you want to know more about me, please find in my [CV]
